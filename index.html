Agentic Foundation Models

The existing agent models inherit the training paradigm that was discovered 3-7 years ago, which was primarily for chat and reasoning (math and coding) scenarios. In the agent era, we need to rethink each stage of such an old paradigm. Agentic foundation models, purposely built for universally-capable agents, redefine the approach of pre-training, mid-training, post-training and large-scale reinforcement learning. The first gigantic use case of agentic foundation models is to push computers into their Fourth Age.
